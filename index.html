PRINCE - I WANNA BE YOUR LOVER

A website dedicated to the best funk-pop song of all time.

I Wanna Be Your Lover, released on August 24, 1979, was the first single from Prince's self-titled release.

On January 8th, 1980, Prince had his first television appearance on a show called Midnight Special. He preformed I Wanna Be Your Lover along with the second single from the same album, Why You Wanna Treat Me So Bad.

Prince would go on to become one of the most influential artists of the modern world due to his androgynous, hyper-sexual aesthetic and his masterful pop sensibilities. He's also known for his relentless work ethic and world class guitar technique.

Notable songs that also contributed to Prince's stardom:

  • When Doves Cry
  • Rasberry Beret
  • Kiss
  • Little Red Corvette
